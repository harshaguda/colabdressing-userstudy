Thank you for your interest in our **Collaborative Dressing** user study.

- Session length: **30 minutes**
- Who can join: Anyone interested in trying and giving feedback

The user study takes place in two phases, in each phase the participants are asked to approach the robot and extend the arm in front of the robot. The experiment is performed with three types of garments, hospital gown, short sleeve shirt, and long sleeve shirt.

The participant can stop the session at any time. And the stop trigger is recorded as part of experimental data.  The robot will have safety mechanisms to stop its operation at any time. A deadman switch is also provided with the participant which can be released anytime they feel unsafe or uncomfortable.

After the interaction with the robot, the participants are asked questions from an already prepared questionnaire. The questionnaire helps us evaluate the perceived safety and comfort of the participants.
